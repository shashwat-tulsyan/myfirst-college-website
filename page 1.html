Jeevandeep Mahavidyalaya

Jeevandeep Mahavidyalaya Affiliated to the Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapeeth University, Varanasi, a Premier Co-educational Institution in the heritage city Varanasi (Ancient Name Kashi) Utter Pradesh, started its journey of spreading higher education in the year of 2012 by Dr. Ashok Kumar Singh. It started its journey with Arts Stream only and now caters number of courses like Degree and Master Courses along with vocational training and Certification courses. The lush green campus is situated centrally located in the city of Varanasi(7 kms away from the Cant Railway Station and Bus Station and 16 kms south to the Airport) and the Institute is affiliated to the Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapeeth University UGC. It is an answer to the long felt need of an English medium college for boys and girls in Varanasi..The college focuses on academic, intellectual and cultural development of its students such that they face the challenges of today with a base firmly rooted in India's cultural heritage. The qualities of fortitude, justice and benevolence are the corner stone of the education imparted here. The college has qualified and motivated faculty, committed to teaching and research Jeevandeep believes in upholding the traditional values. Keeping pace with the same rhythm various cultural activities are being organized in Jeevandeep Mahavidyalaya in order to retain cultural ethos and values. A number of educative, recreational and extra-curricular activities are integral part of the working of the college. In each of the extra-curricularactivities of the college we make sure to include the participation of the students. Such a step gives them in experience in understanding the nittygritty of organizing the events, an experience that would stand them in good stead when they would assume positions of importance. Quizzes, debates, Extempore, Public Speaking are the order of the day the country over and nothing serves to develop a students faculties better.Keeping this in mind the college ensures that students are provided with ample opportunities to participate in all such events. A range of indoor and outdoor games are available students are encouraged to keep fit & healthy by participating in games.
